Why Choose a Certified Pre-Owned Honda?

Honda Certified Used Vehicles are highly sought after thanks to Honda's legendary reputation for durability, quality, and reliability.

Reason 1

In order to earn the distinction of Honda Certified, each used Honda vehicle must pass a series of strict Honda Canada mandated mechanical and appearance inspections.

Reason 2

Only vehicles that meet these rigorous standards are eligible for admission into the Honda Certified Used Vehicle program.

Reason 3

Honda Certified Used Vehicles are offered special discounted finance rates from Honda Financial Services.

Exchange Privilege

Every Honda Certified Used Vehicle offers a no-hassle 7 day / 1,000 km exchange privilege.*

Although we're confident that you will not need to take advantage of this benefit, we offer this to give you peace of mind — if for any reason you are unhappy with your certified vehicle, you may exchange it for another Honda CUV model at the dealership.

View Disclaimer

100 Point Inspection

All Honda Certified Used Vehicles must undergo a thorough Honda Canada mandated 100-point mechanical and appearance inspection by Honda factory-trained technicians.

This bumper-to-bumper inspection helps identify and correct any issues, using only Honda Genuine parts to ensure top quality.

Power Train Warranty

For additional peace of mind, Honda Certified Used Vehicles come with a Honda Canada backed 7 year / 160,000 km Power Train Warranty — an additional 2 year / 60,000 km beyond the original factory Power Train warranty.

Honda Certified Used Vehicles also have the option to upgrade to a Honda Plus Extended Warranty.
